TEDxFargo CHARGE — faithful letter build

Replicating the logo's hollow neon-outline letters, channels widened just enough to fit your Ø12 pixels.

1 · The letters we're replicating

Each letter is a hollow neon outline — the tube traces both edges of every stroke. We keep this exactly.

2 · The only wrinkle: scale — and the fix

Problem: at 300 mm letters the neon tube is only ~12 mm wide. The red dots are your Ø12 pixel, to scale — as fat as the whole line. No room for a pixel + walls.

Fix: widen each tube +3 mm → ~18 mm. The pixel fits, and the hollow-outline look is preserved (the two lines stay separate). Faithful, just a touch chunkier.

3 · The plan & the numbers

  • Letters: ~300 mm tall (bed height), hollow neon outline, tubes widened to ~18 mm. Both lines lit.
  • Construction (validated): white shell + clear/fuzzy lens channel, print lens-up, pixels from behind — same as the bolt.
